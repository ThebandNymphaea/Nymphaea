Journal
14 August 2025

Why the bridge changed

We had a version that worked technically. Every note was where it should be. We played it for three weeks before realising it didn't feel like anything. The version that survived came from a mistake in rehearsal that we kept. The mistake was more honest than the plan.

02 September 2025

On the lyric that stayed

Twenty-two versions of the opening line. The one that made it through was the fourth draft, abandoned for six weeks, returned to by accident. Some things don't get better with effort. They get better with distance.

18 September 2025

The rehearsal that broke everything open

There's a moment when something stops being a song you're practicing and starts being something you're inside. We had that moment on a Tuesday. It doesn't happen often. When it does, you write down what you were doing.
