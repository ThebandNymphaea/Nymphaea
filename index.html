About Nymphaea

Four musicians from Bangalore, writing progressive fusion that holds complexity and feeling in the same hand.

Nymphaea began as a question — whether music built from intricate structure could still arrive somewhere human. Every arrangement is engineered note by note, then played until it breathes. Written for headphones at midnight, built for rooms full of people.
